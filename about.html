About our Hotel

Upscale rooms and wood cottages come with Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs and minibars, as well as safes. Some are situated around the pool or courtyard. Many add furnished terraces, whirlpool tubs and/or sea views. Room service is available..

There's an open-air restaurant, a bar and an outdoor pool and sauna. The funky grounds have hammocks and sculptural wood objects.
